EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

NovaTech Financial Corp reported first-quarter 2024 net revenue of $412.7 million, representing a 14.3% increase year-over-year compared to $361.0 million in Q1 2023. Net income attributable to common shareholders was $87.4 million, or $1.12 per diluted share, up from $71.2 million, or $0.91 per diluted share, in the prior-year period.

CEO Marcus Delano stated: "Q1 2024 reflects the continued momentum of our embedded finance platform. Our payment processing volume surpassed $18 billion for the quarter, and we added 340 new enterprise clients, the strongest single-quarter intake in company history."

OPERATIONAL HIGHLIGHTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- Enterprise client count reached 4,870, up 7.5% from 4,530 at year-end 2023.
- NovaTech's AI-powered fraud detection engine blocked an estimated $340 million in fraudulent transactions during Q1, a 41% improvement over the prior year.
- The company completed the acquisition of Arbor Payments Inc. on February 14, 2024, for $215 million in cash. Arbor processed $2.8B in annualized TPV at time of acquisition and is expected to be immediately accretive to adjusted EPS.
- International revenue grew to $61.9M, representing 15.0% of total revenue, up from 11.4% in Q1 2023, driven primarily by expansion in the UK, Canada, and Australia.
- Monthly active users on NovaTech's consumer app reached 2.1 million, up from 1.6 million at the start of the year.

RISK FACTORS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Management identified the following material risks for the remainder of 2024:

1. Credit Risk: The net charge-off rate in Lending Solutions increased from 1.18% in Q4 2023 to 1.42% in Q1 2024, reflecting modest deterioration in consumer credit quality. Management has tightened underwriting standards for unsecured personal loans originated through third-party channels.

2. Regulatory Risk: Proposed CFPB rules on buy-now-pay-later products, expected to be finalized in mid-2024, could require additional disclosures and impose underwriting requirements that may reduce origination volumes.

3. Integration Risk: The Arbor Payments acquisition introduces integration execution risk. Management expects full system integration to be completed by Q4 2024.

4. Interest Rate Risk: NovaTech carries $1.18B in variable-rate debt. A 100 basis point increase in reference rates would increase annual interest expense by approximately $11.8 million.
