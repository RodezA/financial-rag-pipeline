EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Cornerstone Bank & Trust reported third-quarter 2024 net income of $34.7 million, or $2.14 per diluted share, compared to $29.8 million, or $1.84 per diluted share, in Q3 2023. Total net interest income was $98.4 million, and total non-interest income was $22.1 million.

President and CEO Sandra Fitch commented: "Q3 2024 reflects the benefit of disciplined asset-liability management during a period of rate uncertainty. Our net interest margin expanded to 3.48% following the Federal Reserve's September 2024 rate cut, and our loan portfolio grew 4.1% annualized during the quarter. Credit quality remained exceptional, with non-performing assets at just 0.31% of total assets."

INTEREST RATE SENSITIVITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

As of September 30, 2024, Cornerstone's balance sheet is asset-sensitive. The following table illustrates the estimated impact of parallel rate shocks on net interest income over the next 12 months:

Rate Scenario         NII Change     NII % Change
+200 bps shock:       +$18.4M        +4.7%
+100 bps shock:       +$9.6M         +2.5%
-100 bps shock:       -$11.2M        -2.9%
-200 bps shock:       -$19.8M        -5.1%

Management anticipates one additional 25 bps Federal Reserve rate cut in Q4 2024, followed by two additional cuts in 2025. Under this path, NIM is expected to compress modestly to approximately 3.35%–3.40% by mid-2025 before stabilizing.
